About Us

We Help People In Need Around The World

Community Hope Expansion and Development Action (CHEDA) was founded in the Eastern Town of Addis Ababa, Area of Goro, in 2017 by a group of students from different universities and colleges. The idea emerged after visiting rural areas around the Gambella Region, Western Ethiopia. Founders Gabriel Chieng Tot and Dr. Chuol Ruey Kompuok (an Assistant Professor at Ethiopian Civil Service University) were deeply moved by the plight of children in refugee camps and the broader challenges faced by vulnerable groups in the region.

Our Mission

  • To ensure equal service in the development, humanitarian, peacebuilding, and livelihood sectors.
  • Providing distinguished services to vulnerable orphans, children, and other groups in need of basic human services.
  • Support for refugees, widows, and people with disabilities in all walks of life.

Our Services

We Provide Essential Services To Transform Lives

Educational Support for Vulnerable Children

Providing learning materials and support to orphans, persons with disabilities, and refugee children to ensure access to education.

Medical Assistance for Complex Cases

Coordinating surgeries, medical care, and transportation for children with complicated health issues needing specialized treatment.

Women's Empowerment Initiatives

Supporting widows and marginalized women with training, sewing machines, and entrepreneurial skills to foster self-reliance.
